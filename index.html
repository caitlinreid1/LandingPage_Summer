Summer Maker Camps

Campers explore design thinking using the principles of STEAM (STEM + Art) with the high-tech tools of Rutgers Makerspace. Projects will involve the use of makerspace tools such as 3D printers, laser cutters, vinyl cutter, CNC machines, basic electronics, and hand tools.

  • Ages: Grades 2-12
  • Location: Rutgers Makerspace, Livingston Campus
  • Dates:
    • Grades 2-5: Aug 13-17
    • Grades 6-8: July 23-27
    • Grades 9-12: July 30-Aug 1
  • Times: 9:00am-4:00pm (drop off at 8:30)
  • Commuter

Rutgers Summer Reading Program

Whether your child is 4 or 14, the Rutgers Summer Reading Program is designed to grow your child's confidence in reading and interpreting language. Through fun and interactive lessons, children can gain or enhance a lifelong appreciation for the written word.

  • Ages: 4-18
  • Location: Many locations throughout New Jersey
  • Dates & Times: Vary by location
  • Commuter

Summer Scholars Program

Designed for high-achieving high school students, the Summer Scholars Program allows individuals to earn university credit, studying right beside undergraduates. Students can choose from over 150 courses in a wide range of subjects at one of the nation's top research universities.

  • Ages: Grades 9-12
  • Location: Rutgers University–New Brunswick
  • Dates & Times: Vary by course
  • Commuter

Pre-College Summer Academies

Rutgers University offers a variety of one-week, intensive residential certificate programs designed to introduce high school students to careers in a range of fields. Fields include Engineering, Leadership, TV Production, STEAM, and Medical Imaging Technology.

  • Ages: 16-18
  • Location: Rutgers University–New Brunswick
  • Dates
    • Medical Imaging Technology (June 24-30)
    • Pre-Engineering (July 8-14 or July 15-21)
    • Leadership (July 22-28)
    • TV Production (July 29-August 4)
    • STEAM (August 5-11)
  • Residential

Summer Start
